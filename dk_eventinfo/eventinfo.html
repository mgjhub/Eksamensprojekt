LONGHORN 2023

Konkurrencen vil igen finde sted i den anden weekend af maj (13.-14. maj). Vi forventer dog mange af de konkurrerende hold møder op fredag den 12. og begynder at indrette deres holdområder, eller bare for at have det sjovt. Nedenfor er et midlertidigt program for weekenden, herunder forskellige aktiviteter.

Friday 12th Saturday 13th Sunday 14th
8 AM Event opens for teams to set up camp 10 AM DM in American BBQ Contest starting 11 AM Breakfast for volunteers. Then clean up the area - See you next year!
12 AM Event opens for visitors 10 AM Various stalls opening
12 AM Stalls open (equipment stalls, food stalls, drink stalls etc.) 2 PM Entertainment starts including line-dance, vintage cars and music from the local bands.
2 PM Kids Que competition starts 6 PM Announcing the winner of this years competition
12 PM Event ending for this year
Friday and Saturday will have activities all day for visitors to explore and have fun. So bring your friends and family for a fun day at the event. Activities includes: Bull-Riding Machine - Music - Line Dance - Taste Tests - Vintage Cars.
