WHAT IS LONGHORN?

LongHorn is an annual competition held in Brørup, Denmark. In this competition, contestants can compete against eachother to find out who makes the best BBQ in different categories. However, LongHorn is not just a competition. It's also an event where everyone can come hang out, look at vintage cars, listen to music, be social and of course taste the food. AMERICAN BBQ

In the United States, barbecue refers to a technique of cooking meat outdoors over a fire; this is often called pit barbecue, and the facility for cooking it is the barbecue pit. This form of cooking adds a distinctive smokey taste to the meat; barbecue sauce, while a common accompaniment, is not required for many styles.
